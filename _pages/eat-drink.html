Spend an afternoon relaxing poolside with a cocktail in hand or enjoy a romantic evening with your someone special, enjoying unique and exquisite flavors that are sure to excite the senses and invigorate the mind.

Executive Chef Michael Smerda

An expert in managing the provision of fine-dining resorts, restaurants and country clubs, Chef Michael Smerda boasts over 22-years of progressive cooking experience. Today, Chef welcomes guests with an open palate as the Executive Chef at Sagamore Hotel Miami Beach's signature restaurant Canvas.
