Jason Newsted

{% endregionblock %} {% regionblock welcome_subtitle, tyspe:header %}
Proximity at its Best
{% endregionblock %} {% regionblock welcome_body_text, type:text %}

In partnership with renowned gallery 55Bellechasse, we are thrilled to be presenting an ambitious and extensive solo show by Jason Newsted. The exceptional exhibit is a survey of 12 years of work, along with new, site-specific installations that reflect Newsted’s lifelong commitment to painting inspired by his extensive career as a Grammy-Award winning musician. As the former bassist and vocalist of Metallica, his visual display illustrates the dynamic parallel between music, history and human expressions.

The artist will also be performing live with his ensemble, The Chophouse Band, at our Private Opening on Wednesday, December 5th and the Sagamore Brunch on Saturday, December 8th.

Gillie and Marc Schattner

{% endregionblock %} {% regionblock welcome_body_text, type:text %}

The hotel’s newly-renovated sculpture garden will be inaugurated with award-winning works by Australian public sculpture artists Gillie and Marc Schattner, known for the world’s largest rhino sculpture, The Last Three, in Astor Place. With shows in over 40 cities, the Schattner’s stunning bronze animal sculptures draw inspiration to the political message of equality among people and things. Their work is displayed among prominent galleries around the world, boasting tremendous accolades including the Allen’s People’s Choice Award, and they were finalists of the Archibald Portrait Prize.

Reo

{% endregionblock %} {% regionblock welcome_subtitle, type:header %}
PRESENTED BY ART REPUBLIC
{% endregionblock %} {% regionblock welcome_body_text, type:text %}

The world of art, music and technology collide with REO presented by ART REPUBLIC, a collaboration of graphic design, animation, video and visual projections. REO’s portfolio includes music videos and live performances by Beyoncé, Jay-Z, Travis Scott and other musicians. At The Sagamore Hotel, he’ll create digital video imaging for Art Week using mixed digital media to immerse the public in a transformative experience. REO’s particular edge and ability to elicit emotion though free-flowing movements will create a sense of freedom and sensuality that deeply connects with viewers.

LIVE PAINTING WORLD-ACCLAIMED STREET ARTISTS

Dede and Nitzan Mintz

Israeli street artists Dede and Nitzan Mintz are a collaborative team creating pieces that share their perspective on many subjects. Many of their pieces are fueled by their experience with anxiety and the struggles of living in an urban sphere. For the past decade, they have placed roots in Tel Aviv and other large art capitals of the world.

Discover More

Pimax

Pimax is a French graffiti artist, sculptor, and DJ known for the strong and provocative messages that his work portrays. He lives in Paris, where he adorns the streets with contemporary art and innovative transformations of classic works of art from the eighties.

Discover More

Noty Aroz

Noty Aroz is an artistic duo consisting of childhood friends from Paris, France. Their multidisciplinary work is inspired by pop culture icons and traditional deities. Each piece of their modern pantheon is handmade using materials such as plastic, leather, and invisible ink.

A South Beach classic since the 1940s, The Sagamore Hotel is an all-suite art destination featuring rotating art galleries that are open to the public 24/7. Known for its creative legacy in the art community, it continues to evolve as an incubator of artistic expression. The oceanfront boutique hotel is surrounded by history, culture and the city’s trendiest restaurants, shopping and nightlife, creating the ultimate setting for the ongoing series #SagamoreisArt.
